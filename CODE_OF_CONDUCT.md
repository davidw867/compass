Code of Conduct

Our Pledge

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community.

Compass One exists to help people make better financial decisions through responsible technology. 
We expect all contributors to interact professionally, respectfully, and constructively.

Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Assume good intentions.
- Welcome constructive feedback.
- Focus discussions on improving the project.
- Help create an environment where everyone can learn.

Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or insults.
- Hate speech.
- Threats or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions.
- Publishing private information without permission.

Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, contributions, or participants that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from the project.

Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, please open a private security or moderation issue or contact the maintainers directly.

Our goal is to resolve issues fairly, respectfully, and transparently.
